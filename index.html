OUR STORY

In the heart of New York City, Bakeology was born out of a passion for blending tradition with innovation. Its founder, Emma, grew up in a family of bakers who passed down cherished recipes, but she dreamed of creating something unique— a bakery where science met artistry.

At Bakeology, every treat told the story of the city. Classic bagels were elevated with inventive toppings, croissants came stuffed with flavors from Chinatown to Little Italy, and the signature Broadway Brioche sparkled with edible gold, capturing the glamour of the city that never sleeps.

But Bakeology was more than its menu. Emma made it a space for community, hosting weekly baking classes and a “Taste Test Tuesdays” event where customers could vote on new creations. It became a hub of creativity and connection, proving that in a city of millions, a simple loaf of bread could bring people together.
